## Ecommerce Agentic ai usecases
E-commerce is one of the most mature domains for Agentic AI because almost every business process—marketing, pricing, inventory, customer service, fulfillment, and supply chain—can be automated or augmented by intelligent agents.

# E-Commerce Agentic AI Maturity Model

### Traditional E-Commerce

```text
Customer
   ↓
Website
   ↓
Human Teams
(Marketing, Pricing, Inventory, Support)
```

### Agentic E-Commerce

```text
Customer
   ↓
AI Agents
   ↓
Autonomous Decisions
   ↓
Business Systems
```

# 1. Product Discovery Agent

### Problem

Customers struggle to find products.

### Agent Responsibilities

* Understand customer intent
* Compare products
* Explain features
* Recommend alternatives

Example:

Customer:

> "I need a laptop for AI development under ₹1 lakh."

Agent:

* Identifies requirements
* Searches catalog
* Compares products
* Explains tradeoffs

Business Impact:

* Higher conversion rates
* Lower abandonment
