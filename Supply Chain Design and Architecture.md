## Gi Group-Supply Chain Transformation Leader
Roles and Responsibilities:

1. Strategy & Leadership

Define and lead the global supply chain intelligence and AI strategy, aligned with business priorities and growth objectives
Partner with senior leadership (Supply Chain, Procurement, Operations) to identify value creation opportunities
Drive a transformation roadmap focused on digital, AI, and advanced analytics capabilities
Establish governance, prioritization frameworks, and success metrics for AI and insights initiatives

2. Business Insights Translation

Translate complex supply chain challenges into actionable analytics and AI solutions
Act as a trusted advisor to business leaders, ensuring insights drive tangible outcomes (cost, service, working capital)
Lead the design of decision-support frameworks, dashboards, and predictive models
Enable a shift from reporting to proactive, insight-led decision-making

3. AI & Digital Solution awareness

Drive the development and deployment of AI/ML use cases alongside preferred vendors (forecasting, inventory optimization, OTIF improvement, etc.)
Partner with data science and technology teams to build scalable, reusable solutions and platforms
Oversee end-to-end lifecycle: ideation → prototyping → deployment → adoption → value tracking
Promote automation and intelligent workflows across supply chain processes

4. Value Realization & Impact

Own and track business impact (cost savings, service improvements, inventory reduction, productivity gains)
Ensure solutions are aligned with measurable KPIs such as OTIF, forecast accuracy, backorders, and cost-to-serve
Drive adoption across regions and functions, ensuring sustained value delivery

5. Stakeholder Collaboration & Change Management

Work closely with global, regional, and site teams to embed solutions into day-to-day operations
Lead change management efforts to drive digital adoption and capability building
Act as a connector across functionssupply chain, IT, analytics, and business teams
Build and mentor a high-performing team across analytics, strategy, and solution development
