    Templates:Marketing, Utility, and Authentication
        1. Marketing Templates
                 Purpose: These templates are used for promotional messages aimed at generating awareness, driving sales, and retargeting customers.
                 Examples: Product launches, sales announcements, abandoned cart reminders, and promotional offers.
                 Cost: This category typically incurs the highest per-message cost due to its promotional nature 
        2. Utility Templates
                Purpose: Utility templates are designed for non-promotional, user-requested, or essential updates related to transactions or processes that customers have opted to receive.
                Examples: Order confirmations, shipping updates, appointment reminders, and feedback surveys. These messages are often triggered by user actions or requests 
                Cost: Utility messages are priced moderately compared to marketing templates 
                
        3. Authentication Templates
        Purpose: These templates are used to verify a customer's identity during various stages of their interaction with a business.
        Examples: One-time passwords (OTPs), password reset codes, and two-factor authentication messages.
        Cost: Authentication templates generally have the lowest rate per message, making them cost-effective for businesses
